Hello! I'm Rizqi Anggara, a passionate web and game developer with a creative mindset. I dedicate myself to pushing the boundaries of technology, creating digital works that are both functional and culturally rich.

One of my proudest projects is Bangka Universe, a website dedicated to introducing and preserving the traditional foods of Bangka. With a modern and informative design approach, this platform aims to educate and captivate the younger generation, ensuring that our local culture remains vibrant and appreciated.

In the realm of game development, I created Mr Dirt, a mobile game that has garnered over 10,000 downloads on the Google Play Store. This success, coupled with positive player feedback and a profitable sale, marks a significant achievement in my career and proves that even simple ideas can thrive in the competitive digital market.

I firmly believe that technology is a catalyst for positive change. Every project I undertake is driven by a clear vision and aims to make a meaningful impact. I'm open to collaboration, project partnerships, and continuous learning to further grow both personally and professionally.
